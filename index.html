About Study Buddy

Study Buddy was created as a service-learning project for AP Computer Science Principles. It was inspired by real struggles students face when trying to manage their time and study effectively for exams.

🎯

The Problem

High school students often don't have a structured way to study. Without a plan, most students end up procrastinating or cramming the night before — neither of which leads to good results.

🔬

Our Research

We interviewed students, teachers, and family members. In almost every conversation, the same issue came up — students struggle to study efficiently and manage their time well.

⚙️

How It Works

The app takes your subject, difficulty, available time, and learning style as inputs. It then uses conditional logic to generate a personalized day-by-day study schedule.

🔄

How We Improved It

After getting feedback from peers and family, we added more subjects, expanded the date range options, and made the instructions clearer and easier to follow.
