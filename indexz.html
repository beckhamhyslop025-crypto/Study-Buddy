Meet Study Buddy

A student-built tool designed to help high schoolers stop procrastinating and start studying smarter through personalized, structured study plans.

What is Study Buddy?

A personalized study plan, built around you.

Study Buddy generates a custom day-by-day study schedule based on a student's subject, difficulty level, available time, and learning style. Students answer a few simple questions and receive a clear, actionable plan complete with study techniques and subject-specific tips.

In one sentence: This app creates personalized study plans for struggling students to help them perform better in academics.

Investigation & Research

The Why

Community Need

A problem hiding in plain sight.

The inspiration came from a real person — Beckham's brother Miguel consistently struggled to study efficiently and dealt with serious stress around school. The more we looked into it, the more we realized he wasn't alone. Students everywhere wanted to do well but had no structured system to prepare for tests.

"Students struggle to study efficiently and deal with a lot of stress when it comes to school. All of this limits their academic success and makes life more stressful."

— Study Buddy Team

Root Causes

Why does this keep happening?

No Structured System

Most students study without any plan, leading to procrastination and last-minute cramming that doesn't stick.

Generic Advice

Teachers occasionally offer tips, but the advice is never personalized to the individual student's needs.

Poor Time Management

Without a plan, students don't know how to spread their studying across days, leading to wasted time or missed topics.


Our Research

We asked. People answered.

We conducted interviews with students, teachers, and family members. In every single conversation, the same theme came up: efficiently managing study time is one of the biggest challenges students face.

Key finding: Across all interviews, the inability to plan and manage study time was identified as the single biggest barrier to academic success.

Innovation & Action

The How

The Solution

A virtual study buddy — built from scratch.

We built Study Buddy using Scratch (scratch.mit.edu). The app guides students through a short series of questions about their workload, subject, and time before their assessment — then generates a personalized study plan designed around their specific needs.

Technical Highlights

The AP CSP concepts that made it work.

Variables

User inputs like subject and workload are stored as variables so the app can remember what the student said and build the right plan.

Conditional Statements

if/else logic determines which study recommendation to display based on the student's answers.

Input to Output

Students provide input through their answers, the program processes it, and a custom study plan is the output.


Iterative Process

We built it. We listened. We improved it.

Round 1 — Initial Build

Created the core app in Scratch with basic subject options and a working conditional logic system.

Round 2 — Student & Family Feedback

Added more subjects, widened the date range, and rewrote instructions to be clearer.

Round 3 — Peer Review

Classmates said the app felt plain and hard to navigate. We responded by adding images throughout to give users visual context at each step.

Final Version

8+ subjects, wider date range, clearer instructions, and images added throughout — a significantly more polished experience.

Peer Feedback — What We Changed

  • App felt plain and hard to navigate → Added images throughout for visual context
  • Not enough subject options → Expanded to 8+ subjects
  • Date range too limited → Widened to cover up to 14 days
  • Instructions were confusing → Rewrote all guidance text to be clearer

Study Buddy replaces the stress and guesswork of studying without a plan. Instead of procrastinating until it's too late, students have a structured, personalized schedule that tells them exactly what to do each day with built-in breaks and subject-specific tips.

Personal Growth

What building this taught us.

This project changed how we think about technology. We saw firsthand how a well-designed app can reduce someone's stress, help them succeed, and teach them a skill — time management — that extends far beyond school. Being a technologist means being a problem solver for real people.

Future Scalability

Where Study Buddy could go next.

If we had another semester, here is what we would build next:

Standardized Test Plans

Specific plans for the SAT, ACT, and AP exams with longer timelines and structured strategies.

Practice Problem Links

Direct links to resources for each subject so students have the materials, not just the plan.

Progress Tracking

A way to check off completed sessions and track improvement over time.

The bigger picture: The habit of planning, working in structured sessions, and reviewing weak spots applies to every area of life — not just school. That is what makes Study Buddy more than just an app.
